About Our Team

Meet the passionate individuals behind FutureTech Careers

Our Team

We're the team behind Future Tech Careers and are commited to helping school leavers discover and pursue rewarding careers in the tech industry. Feel free to get in touch with us below.

Prince Kumar

Prince is a gap year student who recently got funding to build an AI B2B start-up creating telecommunications fraud detection software. During high school he built a biotech start-up, published a research paper on climate change, and represented the junior England chess squad internationally. He's going to be starting an undergraduate degree in mathematics at the University of Oxford and hopes to become a tech founder.

Andrew Long

Andrew is a Newcastle college graduate who holds a Level 3 diploma in IT at distinction level; he is a skilled QA tester that has worked with companies and game developers. Other than professional achievements, he has supported foster care and helped in both a technical and internal manner; he has a blend of academic and technical experience. making him impactful in a tech area.

Oliver Noble

Oliver is an aspiring programmer and software engineer, holding a CertHE in Computer Science. In his spare time he enjoys customising his Emacs with Lisp code, volunteering with the Tyne Theatre's restoration project, and listening to music that sounds bad. He hopes to further expand his knowledge of computing and gain more experience in a real-world setting.

Matthew Burton

Matt has been a swimming teacher working with schools, organisations and the general public to deliver a variety of water-based academic programmes, including Swim England's Learn to Swim Programme, School Swimming and Rookie Lifeguards. He has experience in planning and executing academic programmes and analysing their effectiveness and adapting based on student and customer needs. With an enthusiasm for problem solving and a great enjoyment of challenges, he is looking forward to engaging in a future in the tech industry.

Seth Mangundu

Seth is a Luton Sixth Form College graduate with A-levels in Maths, Further Maths and Computer Science at AAB, respectively. He is also a University of Birmingham Summer Award recipient and effective communicator with certifications in debating. Along with academic and technical experience, he has a passion for software development.
